FAQ

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO GET STARTED


HOW DO I GET STARTED?

You can start by calling now or scheduling a time for Ben to call you via email. On our call we will discuss your availability and needs. From there we can schedule your complimentary introductory private yoga session.


WHAT SUPPLIES DO I NEED?

You’ll need a mat, two blocks, a blanket, and a strap. It’s okay if you don’t have everything yet for the introductory session.


I’M JUST LOOKING FOR A TEACHER TO DO A ONE-TIME SESSION WITH. DO YOU DO THAT?

Ben works with clients who are interested in working with a private yoga instructor on a weekly, ongoing basis. We don’t offer single sessions at this time.


I LIVE A LITTLE OUTSIDE THE NEIGHBORHOODS YOU LISTED. WILL YOU TRAVEL TO ME?

Ben mostly work in Marylebone, Carnaby, Soho, Fitzrovia, Tyburnia, and Covent Gardens. If you are slightly outside this zone, we may be able to accommodate with an adjustment to our in rates.


WHAT ARE YOUR RATES?

Private yoga rates are comparable to private psychotherapy rates. We’ll give you a quote on our phone consultation.


I’VE NEVER DONE YOGA BEFORE. IS THIS FOR ME?

Yes! We love working with beginners.


I HAVE AN INJURY/ILLNESS/DISABILITY. CAN YOU WORK WITH ME?

Ben is experienced in teaching yoga to people with a range of health conditions. We’ll discuss your specific needs on our phone call, and make sure you work with a teacher who has the skills to give you the best experience possible.
