Welcome Home

Life is hectic and schedules are impossible. Trying to squeeze a yoga class into your busy day and rushing to a studio ends up creating more stress in your life.

I MAKE IT EASY AND BRING THE YOGA TO YOU.

Your personalised, convenient private yoga program takes place in your London home, with a weekly appointment at the perfect time for you.

Private Yoga London was founded by Ben Hemson-Struthers. Realizing the need for quality in-home yoga lessons amongst London’s busy professionals, Ben shifted focus from group classes and began specializing in private sessions with individuals and couples.

Over the years I have worked with CEOs, doctors, lawyers, producers, psychotherapists, and many more wonderful clients. I am pleased to create a space for professionals to restore physically and spiritually through yoga practice and return to their jobs, families, and personal lives feeling grounded, focused, and clear.

Ben Hemson-Struthers

I was born in London and grew up in the sunny shores of Cape Town. It was in Cape Town at the young age of sixteen that I first found myself in a yoga class. After struggling with depression and substance abuse throughout my adolescence, I found refuge in embodiment and self connection through yoga practice that I hadn't realized I’d been starving for.

I practiced yoga as a student for seven years before becoming a certified instructor in 2015. I have taught yoga to people of all ages from toddlers to seniors, and in all settings from addiction treatment centres to corporations and everywhere in between. I have trained in a variety of mind and body health practices with many wonderful teachers and mentors, and continue to practice as a lifetime student to this day.
